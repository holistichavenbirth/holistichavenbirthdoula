About Me

 

My name is Katie Bartlett and I am a birth doula here in the Treasure Valley. I am deeply passionate about empowering women throughout their childbirth journey. I believe in the power of holistic care, where the mind and body work in harmony. My approach to birth is centered on providing true informed consent, so that every woman no matter their circumstances feels supported and fully in control of her choices. Whether through physical support, emotional and spiritual care, or educational resources, my goal is to guide women to a positive, empowered birth experience.
